Meet Your Doctor
Senior Consultant Dentist
Dr. Faisal Akbani

Dr. Faisal Akbani is a highly accomplished dental professional with over 15 years of dedicated clinical experience. He holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) specialising in Prosthodontics, along with a prestigious Fellowship in Implantology from the International Congress of Oral Implantologists (ICOI).

Renowned for his gentle chairside manner and meticulous attention to detail, Dr. Akbani has successfully transformed thousands of smiles — from routine cleanings to complete smile makeovers. He combines the latest digital dentistry techniques with a deeply patient-centred philosophy, ensuring every individual receives the highest standard of care.

Dr. Akbani is an active member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA), the Academy of Osseointegration, and regularly participates in global dental conferences to stay at the forefront of advancements in implantology and cosmetic dentistry.

Comprehensive Care

Treatments We Offer

We provide a full spectrum of dental treatments using the most advanced techniques and technology available — all under one roof.

🦷

Root Canal Treatment

A pain-free procedure to save severely infected or decayed teeth. Using rotary endodontics and digital imaging, we make root canals comfortable and precise.

🪨

Teeth Fillings

Tooth-coloured composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth, restoring full function and aesthetics without silver amalgam.

😬

Dentures

Custom-crafted full and partial dentures that fit comfortably and look completely natural — restoring your smile, bite, and confidence.

🦴

Dental Implants

Titanium implants that permanently replace missing teeth. Guided surgery ensures precision placement for lasting results that look and feel completely natural.

📐

Teeth Braces

Metal and ceramic braces designed to correct misaligned teeth and bite issues. Our orthodontic plans are tailored to achieve the ideal result for every patient.

🔲

Clear Aligners

Virtually invisible, removable aligners (Invisalign® & others) that straighten teeth discreetly — perfect for adults and teens wanting braces without the look.

✨

Teeth Cleaning

Professional scaling, polishing, and deep cleaning to remove tartar, stains, and bacteria — the foundation of a healthy, bright, long-lasting smile.

👑

Tooth Crown

Porcelain and zirconia crowns that restore damaged, cracked, or weakened teeth to full strength while matching the shape and shade of your natural teeth.

🔗

Dental Bridges

Fixed dental bridges that seamlessly fill the gap left by missing teeth, restoring your bite, smile, and preventing remaining teeth from shifting.

🩺

Tooth Extraction

Gentle, virtually painless extractions — including surgical removal of impacted wisdom teeth — performed with care to ensure quick, comfortable recovery.

🫁

Gingivitis Treatment

Diagnosis and treatment of gum disease at every stage — from mild gingivitis to advanced periodontitis — to protect your gum health and prevent tooth loss.

🌟

Smile Makeover

Bespoke cosmetic treatment plans combining veneers, whitening, bonding, and contouring — crafted using Digital Smile Design technology to preview your new smile.
